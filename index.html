What we do

We are building a digital-native, multi-sided platform focused on kids’ activities.

The problem

Fragmented discovery for parents

Parents struggle to discover safe, age-appropriate, and engaging activities for their kids. Information is scattered across social media, WhatsApp groups, and flyers, and bookings are often manual and unreliable.

Our solution

A unified home for kids’ activities

  • Parents can discover, compare, and book activities in a few taps.
  • Hosts can list activities, manage enrollments, and receive payments.
  • Over time, data helps match each child with the right activities.

Target audience: parents of kids aged 2–16 and activity providers (coaches, academies, studios, community centers) in India initially, expanding globally.

How it works

For parents

From search to enrollment in minutes

  1. Sign up and add one or more child profiles.
  2. Discover curated activities based on age, interests, and location.
  3. Book and pay directly through the platform.
  4. Track schedules, attendance, and progress in your dashboard.
For hosts

Grow your activity business

  1. Create a host profile and list your activities.
  2. Set schedules, capacity, and pricing.
  3. Accept bookings and manage rosters.
  4. Get paid reliably and view performance insights.
